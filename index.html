Tour Highlights

DAY 1
PAG-ASA
Located at PAG-ASA Weather Station Subic Bay Freeport Zone

We visit the Philippine Atmospheric Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration (PAG-ASA),
where a representative explained how they measure wind and heat index using various of tools. Their work is
crucial for aviation, as they provide vital information about upcoming strong winds and other weather conditions
that can affect flight paths.

DAY 1
First Aviation Academy
Located at Subic Bay Freeport Zone in Zambales

After visiting PAG-ASA, we proceeded to the First Aviation Academy, which is the
first school to offer pilot training and other courses related to aviation.

DAY 1
Port of Subic Bay
Located at Subic Bay Freeport Zone in Zambales

The Subic Bay Port handles a wide range of cargo, including containerized goods, bulk cargo, and more.
it also has facilities for ship repair and maintenance.

DAY 2
QBO Innovation Hub
Located at Draper Startup House for Entrepreneurs Manila 3/F Clock-In by AyalaLand Offices Makati Stock Exchange Building 6767 Ayala Avenue, Makati City Metro Manila, Philippines

In day 2 of tour we visit the QBO company. QBO company helps promoting and supporting
growth of startups in the Philippine by them providing the necesarry tools, networks, and
resources to succeed.

DAY 2
Packetworx IOT Technology Hub
Located at The Ignacia Place 62 Sgt. Esguerra Avenue, Diliman, Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines

Packetworx builds and operates IOT(internet of things) using various technologies
such as LoRaWAN(Long Range Wide Area Network) and NB-IoT(Narrowband IoT).

DAY 3
Light Rail Transit Authority-Line 2
Located at Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA) Line 2 Depot, Marcos Highway, Santolan Pasig City, Metro Manila, Philippines

LRTA line 2 provides accessible and efficient travel for thousands of commuters daily. It utilizes
elevated tracks for most of its route, ensuring efficient transportation.

DAY 3
National Museum of the Philippines
Located at Padre Burgos Avenue, Ermita Manila, Metro Manila, Philippines

After visiting the LRTA-line 2, we proceededto the National Museum of the Philippines, where we
explored our national cultures and treasures. We viewed paintings by a well-known Filipino artist.

DAY 4
98 Labs Inc
Unit 1806, Hanston Square Building 17 San Miguel Avenue, Ortigas Center Pasig City, Metro Manila, Philippines

98 Labs Inc. is a dynamic tech company known for its innovative solutions and cutting-edge software development.
The guest speaker taught us that we need to believe in ourselves and everything can be solve.

DAY 4
Hytec Power Inc
Unit 1807, Cityland Pasong Tamo Tower 2210 Chino Roces Avenue, Makati City Metro Manila, Philippines

Hytec Power Inc. is recognized for its expertise providing reliable power solutions and tools for school to use.
They specialized in delivering efficient and sustainable machines in industrial needs.

DAY 5 and 6
Baguio
Baguio City

During the rest of our tour, we visited Baguio over the weekend. We explored the city and started with a trip to the Strawberry Farm, where we bought some souvenirs. Next, we visited the PMA Academy and then enjoyed Mines View Park, and we ended our day at the night market. Speakers

1st day Speakers

Speaker: Sir. Arnel G. Gonzales

Sir Arnel from PAG-ASA explained the various equipment and tools used to measure different types of upcoming weather. He also emphasized the importance of monitoring the weather, as the information is directly communicated to NAIA to ensure flight safety.

Speaker: Sir. Dave of Sales and Marketing

Sir Dave explained how their academy was the first to offer pilot courses and related fields like aeronautics engineering. He also gave us a tour of the academy, showcasing its impressive facilities, including the Redbird simulator used for pilot training.

Speaker: Engineer. Jereme John Quiroz

Engineer Jereme taught us about the operations at Subic Port. He explained that it mainly involves handling cargo, communicating with other ships via satellite using specialized equipment, and performing ship repairs and maintenance.

2nd day Speakers

Speaker: Ma'am Carla Legazpi

Ma'am Carla from QBO Hub explained how their company helps startups grow from scratch by providing them with the necessary materials and resources to succeed

Speaker: Sir John of Packetworx

Sir John explained how the Internet of Things (IoT) works and how their company ensures satisfaction for their providers by delivering high-quality services and using top-notch equipment. He also mentioned that their company is continuously working on enhancing their IoT capabilities.

3rd day Speakers

Speaker: Sir Adrian Saldaña

Sir Adrian gave us a tour inside the LRT Line 2 and explained how the trains are built, repaired, and cleaned. He also provided details about the use of electricity to power the trains, mentioning that they use their own power supply from Meralco. Additionally, he showed us the operations and monitors, where they maintain contact with the train conductors.

4th day Speakers

Speaker: Sir Brian Tan Seng

Sir Brian shared how their company started and how it grew over time. He also gave us valuable wisdom and an inspiring speech, sharing a bit about his life. He told us how he started from scratch and became a successful entrepreneur and programmer.

Speaker: Ma'am Mary Grace Fajardo

Ma'am Grace gave us a tour of Hytec Power, where they showcased their technology and machinery. She explained how all the tools and machines are used
